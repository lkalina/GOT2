Queen Cersei Lannister is a major character in the first, second, third, fourth,fifth, and sixth seasons. She is portrayed by starring cast member Lena Headeyand debuts in the series premiere. Cersei is the widow of King Robert Baratheonand Dowager Queen of the Seven Kingdoms. She is the daughter of Lord Tywin Lannister, twin sister of Jaime Lannister and elder sister of Tyrion Lannister. She has an incestuous sexual relationship with Jaime, who is secretly the father of her three children, Joffrey, Myrcella and Tommen.
