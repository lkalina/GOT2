Jaime Lannister is a major character in the first, second, third, fourth, fifth andsixth seasons. He is played by starring cast member Nikolaj Coster-Waldau and debuts in the series premiere.

Ser Jaime Lannister is a knight of the Kingsguard, the eldest son of Tywin Lannister, younger twin brother of Queen Cersei Lannister, and older brother ofTyrion Lannister. Previously he served in the Kingsguard of Aerys II Targaryenand, infamously, betrayed and killed Aerys during the Sack of King's Landing, earning him the sobriquet "Kingslayer". Jaime is involved in an incestuoussexual relationship with Cersei, and, unknown to most, is the biological father of her three children, Joffrey, Myrcella and Tommen.
