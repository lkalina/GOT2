King Stannis Baratheon is a major character in the second, third, fourth andfifth seasons. He is played by starring cast member Stephen Dillane, and debuts in "The North Remembers". Stannis is mentioned in the first season but did not appear. He is the Lord of Dragonstone, the younger brother of Robert Baratheonand older brother of Renly Baratheon.
