Arya Stark is a major character in the first, second, third, fourth, fifth and sixthseasons. She is played by starring cast member Maisie Williams, and debuts in the series premiere. Arya is the third child and second daughter of Lord Eddard Stark and his wife Lady Catelyn Stark.
